Can a customer make changes to a order that has already been placed?

Yes, they can. But if the product they change their order to is more expensive than the last one, they will have to pay the remainding fee due for that product.

How can an order be made?

A customer can make an order online via the company website or they can go in-store one of the branches closes to them to make an order.

How can an order be cancelled?

If an order is already made and paid for, it cannot be cancelled.However, the individual can choose another product which they desire to exchange their last one for.

Is Red-Riot Technologies products purchased with one time payment?

Yes, a customer can only make a payment once to get a product.

Can a customer get a refund after purchasing a product ?

No, they can only exchange that product for another one.
